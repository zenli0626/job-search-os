# STAR Story Bank

<!-- HOW TO FILL THIS IN
Each story = one discrete work accomplishment formatted in STAR structure.
Aim for 8–12 stories covering the themes your target roles care about.
Be specific: real numbers, real timelines, real stakeholders (by role, not name).
Stories are PRIVATE — keep real details here; the Career Brain will mask on public surfaces.

Common themes to cover:
- Leading through ambiguity
- Cross-functional alignment
- Data-driven decision making
- Process improvement / scale
- Difficult stakeholder / conflict
- Failure and recovery
- Building something new
- Managing / developing people
-->

**Situation:**
[2–3 sentences. What was the context? What was the problem or opportunity? Be specific about the stakes.]

**Task:**
[1–2 sentences. What was YOUR specific responsibility? Not the team's — yours.]

**Action:**
[3–5 sentences. What did YOU specifically do? Steps, decisions, who you influenced, how.

**Result:**
[1–2 sentences. What happened? Include metrics where they exist.
